What is Calendie?

Calendie is a WhatsApp-based calendar assistant that lets you schedule, cancel, and check appointments by simply chatting. No apps, no downloads – just message your calendar like you would a friend.

How It Works

  • Schedule Events: Just type something like "Book meeting for Friday at 3PM."
  • Cancel Appointments: Type "Cancel Monday meeting."
  • Check Availability: Ask "What's on my calendar tomorrow?"
  • 100% WhatsApp: No apps or accounts needed — all through chat.
